About Us

Tyme Vietnam

Established in 2015, Tyme Vietnam has been the international arm of Tyme, gathering our engineering and product teams, who share a global mission to become smart bank builders and reimagine the bank's digital future through technology. We strive hard to find the right balance between the digital and physical worlds: the healthy mix that boosts productivity and employee wellbeing.

Ownership

Ownership is the integral driving force that enables our bank builders to innovate and build great customer solutions.

Respect

We empower our people to be their best and treat everyone as an entrepreneur with a voice to be heard.

Lean

We believe in working smart, being resourceful, and continuously optimizing.

Transparent

Transparency builds trust. We value open communication, honesty, and integrity in all that we do.

Improving

We are committed to learning, growing, and continuously improving our products and processes.
